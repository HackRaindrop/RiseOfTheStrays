# Cat Stats and Their Effects on Attributes

This document outlines how each stat affects a cat's attributes in Rise of the Strays.

## STR - Paw Power
- **Physical Attack**: +1.5 per point
- **Physical Defense**: +0.5 per point
- **Attack Damage**: +1 per point
- **Push Force**: +0.05 per point

## DEX - Whisker Dexterity
- **Accuracy**: +1% per point
- **Speed**: +0.5 per point

## AGI - Tail Balance
- **Dodge Chance**: +0.75% per point
- **Attack Speed**: +0.025 per point
- **Jump Height**: +0.05 per point

## VIT - Clawstitution
- **Max Health**: +5 per point
- **Health Regeneration**: +0.2 per point

## WIL - Fur-titude
- **Magic Resistance**: +0.75% per point
- **Debuff Resistance**: +0.5% per point
- **Casting Time Reduction**: +0.25% per point

## INT - Meowmental
- **Magic Attack**: +1.5 per point
- **XP Gain Multiplier**: +0.025 (2.5%) per point

## CHA - Charm
- **Synergy Bonus**: +0.5% per point (capped at 15%)
- **Recruit Chance Bonus**: +0.75% per point
- **Bonding Speed**: +0.05 per point
- **Happiness Bonus**: +0.3% per point

## PER - Purrception
- **Critical Hit Chance**: +0.2% per point (1% per 5 points)
- **Stealth Detection Range**: +0.1 per point

## LCK - Luck
- **Rare Drop Chance**: +0.25% per point
- **Status Effect Avoidance**: +0.15% per point
- **Happiness Luck Bonus**: +0.2% per point

## Cat Types and Stat Bonuses

Each cat type receives bonuses to specific stats:

### Scavenger
- **Primary Stats**: PER, LCK (+3 each)
- **Secondary Stats**: DEX, AGI (+1 each)
- **Description**: Treasure hunter, item finder, stealthy and fast.

### Hunter
- **Primary Stats**: AGI, DEX (+3 each)
- **Secondary Stats**: STR, PER (+1 each)
- **Description**: Agile attacker with high crit and precision.

### Guardian
- **Primary Stats**: VIT, STR (+3 each)
- **Secondary Stats**: WIL, CHA (+1 each)
- **Description**: Tanky, protects team, high HP and defense.

### Medic
- **Primary Stats**: INT, WIL (+3 each)
- **Secondary Stats**: CHA, DEX (+1 each)
- **Description**: Healer/support cat, uses intellect and calm will.

### Mystic
- **Primary Stats**: INT, WIL (+3 each)
- **Secondary Stats**: LCK, PER (+1 each)
- **Description**: Magical or energy-based, status effects and spells.

### Trickster
- **Primary Stats**: LCK, CHA (+3 each)
- **Secondary Stats**: DEX, INT (+1 each)
- **Description**: Tricky and charming, good with luck-based abilities.

### Beast
- **Primary Stats**: STR, AGI (+3 each)
- **Secondary Stats**: VIT, WIL (+1 each)
- **Description**: Strong physical attacker with good mobility.

### Diplomat
- **Primary Stats**: CHA, INT (+3 each)
- **Secondary Stats**: WIL, PER (+1 each)
- **Description**: Social and intelligent, good at recruiting and bonding.

### Shadowpaw
- **Primary Stats**: AGI, PER (+3 each)
- **Secondary Stats**: DEX, LCK (+1 each)
- **Description**: Stealthy and perceptive, good at critical hits.

### Tinkerer
- **Primary Stats**: INT, DEX (+3 each)
- **Secondary Stats**: PER, WIL (+1 each)
- **Description**: Crafty and precise, good with technical tasks.

### Wanderer
- **All Stats**: +1 to all stats
- **Description**: Balanced stats, no specific focus.

## Synergy Bonus

When cats are grouped together, they receive a synergy bonus based on the average Charisma (CHA) of all cats in the group. This bonus applies to:
- Physical Attack
- Physical Defense
- Magic Attack
- Magic Resistance
- Health Regeneration

The synergy bonus is calculated as: 1 + (average CHA * 0.5%) with a cap of 15%.
